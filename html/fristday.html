Sadabahar Flower

The term Sadabahar is the Hindi name for a popular flowering plant called Periwinkle in English. The scientific name for this plant is Catharanthus roseus. It is known for its attractive and colourful flowers, which come in shades of pink, purple, and white.

Kanakambaram Flower

The flower known as Kanakambaram in Hindi is commonly referred to as Marigold in English. Marigolds belong to the genus Tagetes and are known for their vibrant and cheerful blooms. They come in various shades of yellow,orange, and maroon, and are often used in floral arrangements, garlands, and decorations.

Sampangi Flower

The flower known as Sampangi in Hindi is commonly called Magnolia in English. The scientific name for this flower is Magnolia champaca. It’s a fragrant flower native to Southeast Asia and is highly valued for its beautiful and aromatic blooms.
Sampangi or Magnolia is often used in perfumes, incense, and religious ceremonies. The flowers are usually yellow or orange in colour and have a unique and captivating fragrance.

Bijli Flower

The flower named Bijli in Hindi refers also to the English flower name Marigold. The difference is that Bijli flowers typically are white in colour.

Champa Flower

The Champa flower is commonly known as the Plumeria or Frangipani flower in English. Plumeria is a tropical flowering plant native to the Caribbean, Central America, and Asia. It is known for its beautiful and fragrant flowers that come in various colours, including white, yellow, pink, and red. The flowers are often used in making perfumes and religious offerings.

Mogra Flower

The Hindi word Mogra is commonly known as Jasmine in English. Jasmine is a fragrant flowering plant that belongs to the genus Jasminum. It is known for its small, white, star-shaped flowers and sweet, intoxicating scent. Jasmine is widely cultivated for its ornamental value and its flowers are used in making perfumes, essential oils, and teas. The fragrance of jasmine is highly cherished, and it is often associated with love, beauty, and spirituality.

Bela Flower

Like Mogra, he flower Bela is also known as Jasmine in English. So, Bela is the Hindi name for the Jasmine flower.

Orchid Flower

The orchid in Hindi is “ऑर्किड फूल” (pronounced as Orkid Phool). Orchids are native to various parts of the world and are found in different regions including tropical rainforests, temperate forests, and even deserts. Orchids are known to be particularly diverse and abundant in tropical regions such as Southeast Asia, Central and South America, and parts of Africa.
